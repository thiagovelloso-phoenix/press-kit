About Phoenix Flight Industries

Phoenix Flight Industries is a Houston-based aerospace company founded in 2021. The company designs and manufactures hydrogen fuel cell-powered UAVs for utility inspection, oil and gas operations, and security monitoring. Its flagship aircraft, the Phoenix P1, is powered by the IE SOAR 2.4kW Hybrid Fuel Cell system and operates across three tank configurations delivering 1–2, 3–4, or 5–6 hours of continuous flight. The P1 is NDAA compliant and designed for organizations whose inspection missions exceed the range of battery-powered platforms. Fulfillment lead time is approximately four months from confirmed order.
